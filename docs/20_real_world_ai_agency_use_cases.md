## 1. Intelligent Customer Support Chatbot Platform

### Business Context
Enterprise clients need 24/7 customer support that handles complex queries across multiple channels with human-like responses.

### Technical Implementation
- **Frontend:** React/Vue.js with real-time chat interface
- **Backend:** Node.js/FastAPI with WebSocket support
- **AI Engine:** GPT-4/Llama 3 for natural language processing
- **Database:** PostgreSQL for conversation history, Neo4j for knowledge graphs
- **Integration:** CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot), ticketing systems (Zendesk)

### Business Value
- 60% reduction in response time
- 40% decrease in support costs
- 24/7 multilingual support availability
